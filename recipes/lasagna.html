How to make a Lasagna

Description

This is a brief lasagna tutorial for cooking a simple recipe. Lasagna is a classic italian dish that consists in layers of flat pasta called lasagna noodles. In this recipe, it's going to be cooked with it's usual sauce; tomato-based, cheese and meat fillings. It's possible that the world "lasagna" comes from the greek λάσανα (lasana) or λάσανον (lasanon), meaning 'trivet', 'stand for a pot' or 'chamber pot'.
